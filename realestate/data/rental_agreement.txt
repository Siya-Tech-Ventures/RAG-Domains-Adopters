1. PROPERTY
The Landlord agrees to rent to the Tenant the residential property located at:
789 Rental Avenue, Apartment 4B
City, State 12345
(hereinafter referred to as the "Property")

2. TERM
The term of this lease shall be for 12 months, beginning on January 1, 2024, and ending on December 31, 2024.

3. RENT
The Tenant agrees to pay monthly rent of $2,000 on or before the 1st day of each month. Late payments may be subject to a fee of up to 5% of the monthly rent.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Tenant shall pay a security deposit of $2,000 upon signing this agreement. The deposit shall be returned within 30 days after the termination of this agreement, less any deductions for damages beyond normal wear and tear.

5. UTILITIES
The Tenant shall be responsible for payment of all utilities including electricity, gas, water, internet, and trash removal.

6. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
The Landlord shall maintain the Property in good repair and make necessary repairs within a reasonable time after notification by the Tenant. The Tenant shall keep the Property in clean and sanitary condition.

7. ALTERATIONS
The Tenant shall not make any alterations, additions, or improvements to the Property without the prior written consent of the Landlord.

8. OCCUPANCY
The Property shall be occupied only by the Tenant and immediate family members. The Tenant shall not sublet the Property without written consent from the Landlord.

9. PETS
No pets are allowed on the Property without prior written consent from the Landlord.

10. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this agreement with 60 days written notice. The Tenant shall be responsible for rent payments through the end of the notice period.

11. DEFAULT
If the Tenant fails to pay rent when due or violates any other term of this agreement, the Landlord may terminate this agreement with 30 days notice.

12. QUIET ENJOYMENT
The Landlord covenants that the Tenant shall peacefully and quietly enjoy the premises during the lease term.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this agreement on [Date].
